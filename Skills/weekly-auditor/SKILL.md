# Weekly Auditor

## Role
Tu AI Employee ka Audit specialist hai. Har hafte ka complete activity
report banata hai — kya kaam hua, kya fail hua, health score.

---

## Script
`Skills/orchestrator/scripts/weekly_audit.py`

## Output
`Plans/WEEKLY_AUDIT_YYYYMMDD.md`

---

## Audit Includes

- Total actions logged (7 days)
- Emails sent / failed
- LinkedIn / WhatsApp / Social posts
- Done/ folder breakdown
- Email success rate
- System health checklist
